1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Content

When you use Study Decoder, you may upload PDF files containing lecture slides or study materials.

  • These files are processed solely to generate exam-style study questions.
  • Uploaded PDF content is processed using third-party AI services (e.g., hosted language models). Personal data is transmitted off-device only for the purpose of generating study questions, and this occurs only after the user explicitly initiates the upload.
  • Uploaded PDFs are deleted immediately after processing and are not stored.

b. Usage Data

We may collect limited, anonymous usage data to understand how the App is used (e.g., feature usage, error events). This data does not identify you personally.

c. Payment Information

All payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple through In-App Purchases. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Process uploaded PDFs and generate study questions
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Manage subscriptions and access to paid features (via Apple)

3. AI Processing

Uploaded content is processed using third-party artificial intelligence services to generate exam-style questions and study insights.

  • Content is sent only for the purpose of processing
  • Content is not used to train models on our behalf
  • Content is deleted immediately after processing

4. Data Retention

  • Uploaded PDFs: Deleted immediately after processing
  • Usage analytics: Retained in aggregated or anonymized form only

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties, except as necessary to operate the App (e.g., AI processing services, Apple subscription services).

6. Children’s Privacy

Study Decoder is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
